Everything you need, nothing you don't

  • 5-hour & 7-day

    Both usage windows at a glance, with a live countdown to each reset.

  • Claude Code + Codex

    Track both providers and multiple accounts — each account gets its own tray icon you can toggle.

  • Right where you need it

    Pops from a screen corner or the tray — click or hover. Pin it to keep it open; it remembers that.

  • Private by design

    Uses your existing local Claude/Codex login — never your password — and talks only to the providers' own endpoints. Nothing else leaves your machine.

One look, four themes

Match your desktop, light or dark.
